Cooking Recipe for Garlic Butter Pasta


Ingredients

  • 200g of spaghetti or any pasta
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: Chopped parsley, grated parmesan, chili flakes

Instructions

  1. Cook the Pasta

    Boil the pasta in salted water according to the package instructions. Reserve ¼ cup of the pasta water before draining.

  2. Make the Garlic Butter Sauce

    In a pan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 1–2 minutes until fragrant (don’t let it burn).

  3. Toss the Pasta

    Add the cooked pasta to the pan with the garlic butter. Pour in a bit of the reserved pasta water to help coat the noodles.

  4. Season & Serve

    Season with salt, pepper, and (optional) chili flakes. Top with parsley or parmesan if you like.
